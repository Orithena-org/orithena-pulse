About Orithena Pulse: Agentic AI

What is Orithena Pulse?

Orithena Pulse is a daily digest of the most important AI developments, curated automatically by the Orithena intelligence pipeline. Each day, Pulse scrapes dozens of sources, deduplicates and scores every item for quality and relevance, then publishes a single page of signal — no filler, no engagement bait, just the developments that matter.

Methodology

The pipeline runs in four stages:

  1. Scrape — Adapters pull content from Hacker News, arxiv, GitHub Trending, and curated RSS feeds.
  2. Deduplicate — URL normalization, title similarity, and arxiv ID matching eliminate duplicate coverage.
  3. Score — A composite score (0–10) based on novelty, relevance, community signal, and source authority determines what makes the cut.
  4. Publish — Items that pass the quality threshold are grouped into sections and rendered as a static site.

Sources

  • Hacker News — Top stories filtered by AI/ML keywords
  • arxiv — New papers in cs.AI, cs.LG, cs.CL, and related categories
  • GitHub Trending — Repositories gaining traction in AI/ML
  • RSS Feeds — Curated blogs and publications (OpenAI, Anthropic, DeepMind, etc.)

Open Source

Orithena Pulse is part of the Orithena project — an open platform for AI-assisted content intelligence. The full source code, including scrapers, scoring logic, and this site generator, is available on GitHub.
